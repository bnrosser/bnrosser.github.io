Military Experience 

Marine Corps veteran with four years experience as an MOS 6156 airframe mechanic on the MV-22 Osprey aircraft. I worked as an aircraft mechanic for four years, inspecting, repairing, and maintaining hydraulic systems, airframe structures, pneumatic systems, tire/wheel assemblies, and brake systems, as well as performing carbon fiber repairs, and applying paint markings and touch-ups. Completed organizational maintenance courses and training consisting of resident schools and follow on training with courses in corrosion identification, treatment, and prevention, and aircraft paint, touch-up, and markings.
